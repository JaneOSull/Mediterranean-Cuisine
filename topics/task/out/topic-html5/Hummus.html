Hummus

This creamy, rich hummus is made using just 5 ingredients and is ready in 10 minutes. Serve with crunchy seasonal veg or warm pitta breads

Hummus is rich in healthy fats, vitamins and minerals

  1. You will need:
    OptionDescription
    400g can chickpeas
    80ml extra virgin olive oil
    1 - 2 garlic cloves
    1 lemon
    3tbsp tahini
  2. Drain and thoroughly rinse the chickpeas in a colander under cold running water.
  3. Tip into the large bowl of a food processor along with 60ml of the oil and blitz until almost smooth.
  4. Add the garlic, lemon and tahini along with 30ml water.
  5. Blitz again for about 5 mins, or until the hummus is smooth and silky.
  6. Add 20ml more water, a little at a time, if it looks too thick.
  7. Season and transfer to a bowl.
  8. Swirl the top of the hummus with the back of a dessert spoon and drizzle over the remaining oil.
